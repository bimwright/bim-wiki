## Paraphrase policy

Where a wiki page presents a definition, clause statement, or requirement originally
expressed in a copyrighted standard (notably ISO 19650), that statement has been
**rewritten in bimwright's own words**, typically in Vietnamese. The page still cites
the source clause (e.g., "ISO 19650-1:2018 §3.3.1") so readers can consult the
normative original.

We do not claim our paraphrase is ISO-authoritative. For contractual or normative
use, refer to the official ISO publication.
